Our Story

History


Our Daata Vaishno Dhaba was started by our grandfather at Nandni Tunnel Janakha. It was very famous in J&K for the dish Rajama Rice with Desi Ghee. After my grandfather, my father put all his efforts into making this Dhaba famous. Later, my father opened this Dhaba at a new destination (Suketar Domail) because of the old highway closure and to enhance business to the peak. We introduced many more Indian food dishes and now, I, Gagneet Singh, along with my father, are running this Dhaba. Our food quality and taste rating are 4.7+ and this is one of the famous Dhabas of J&K. Now we are working hard to become J&K's first tastiest and most famous Dhaba.
